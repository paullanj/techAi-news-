Big Oil powers AI data centers Exxon Mobil plans a natural-gas-powered plant to supply a data center, paired with carbon capture targeting ~90% emissions reduction. [1] Chevron is assessing similar opportunities leveraging gas production, power equipment, and land for potential sites. [1] Tech majors are exploring nuclear, but oil companies argue natural gas is a faster near-term solution for AI’s power needs. [1] AI-first future for oil & gas AI is reducing analysis cycles, predicting equipment failures, and accelerating workflows across the value chain. [2] Early adopters could achieve 30%–70% incremental EBIT uplift over five years. [2] Use cases include autonomous maintenance scheduling, real-time field guidance, and emissions monitoring. [2] Oil & gas in climate-tech partnerships Partnerships between O&G firms and climate-tech startups (e.g., geothermal drilling) are increasing to support the energy transition.
